# hi, I’m Sarah 👋

I’m a digital infrastructure manager at a civic nonprofit, which is a very long way of saying: I make the tools talk to each other so my people can do the work, while constantly discovering that “digital infrastructure” apparently means whatever is on fire today.

I work in the sacred nonprofit tradition of “we do not have enterprise money, but we do have a deadline,” which means a lot of my job is figuring out what broke, where the data went, who owns the thing, and how to make it work anyway.

I live every day wondering if future me is going to hate me for the decisions. I document aggressively while my ADHD turns every “quick question” into a seven-part investigative series.

I’m basically three raccoons in a trench coat with a mug of Portland Breakfast, a suspiciously large notes doc, and the audacity to ask, “Wait... where the hell did this come from?”
